# Clinirisk-pad
Clinical decision support tool for PAD risk stratification in diabetic patients
ClinRisk-PAD — Peripheral Arterial Disease Risk Stratification Tool
An open-source clinical decision support tool that identifies diabetic patients at highest risk of progression to critical limb-threatening ischemia and lower limb amputation.
Built by Adesoye Taiwo Emmanuel — Independent Researcher, OAU Nigeria.
Clinical variables assessed: HbA1c, Ankle-Brachial Index (ABI), Serum Creatinine
Output: Three-tier risk classification (HIGH RISK, MODERATE RISK, Low risk) with recommended clinical action and exportable dated CSV report.
Mission: To prevent limb amputation in high-risk diabetic patients through early computational risk identification.
